Bicyclists in Seattle face danger, but conditions are improving

By: Sammi Bushman

In 2019, Seattle had 29 vehicle involved bike accidents

2 were fatal

While this may seem like a lot, Seattle has seen a drastic decrease in bicycle involved accidents over the past decade
